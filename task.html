Christmas

The day of the week of Christmas depends on the day of the week of the first day of December. For example if December 1 is Sunday, Christmas will be on a Tuesday. You will write a program to figure out the day of the week of Christmas when you are given the day of December 1. You should use integer math to solve this problem.

Note: In computer science, lists generally start at index 0, rather than 1. For this lab, Sunday will correspond to 0, Monday to 1, and so on. Saturday will correspond to 6.

Assignment:

1. Write a program to find the day of the week of Christmas when given the day of the week of December 1.
You will only need to use two math operators in your algorithm.
We will need to use the variables dayOfChristmasString and startDayOfTheMonthString later in this program, so you will need to declare their type.

2. Verify that the program gives the correct day of the week for each starting day of December, Sunday through Monday.

3. Make sure your name is commented out near the top of your source code.
